Noise Types & Benefits

White Noise

Equal energy across all frequencies. Sounds like TV static. Masks ambient sounds effectively. Great for babies and office focus.

Pink Noise

Decreasing energy at higher frequencies (1/f). Sounds like rain or a stream. Boosts focus and creativity. Promotes alpha brain waves.

Brown Noise

Even stronger low frequencies (1/f²). Sounds like a waterfall or strong wind. Deep sleep and relaxation. Reduces stress and anxiety.

Pure Tone

Single-frequency sine wave (20Hz-20kHz). Adjustable frequency. Used for tinnitus masking, hearing tests, and meditation.
